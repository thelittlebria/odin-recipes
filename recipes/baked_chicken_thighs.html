Baked Chicken Thighs

Description

This is a delicious baked chicken breast recipe, super simple - just 4 ingredients and 3 steps.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Place the chicken in an oven pan.
  3. Put the chicken in the oven at 375 Fahrenheit.
