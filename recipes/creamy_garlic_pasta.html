Creamy Garlic Pasta

Description

5 simple ingredients, follow some easy steps, and you've got a yummy in your tummy quick pasta!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Half and half or heavy whipping cream
  • Butter - we love butter
  • Flour - to make it a lil' thiccc

    • Optional

  • Parsley
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil your noodles, make sure to set a timer to your appropriate preference of tenderness!
  2. Melt butter in pan.
  3. Once butter is melted, add in garlic and mix it for about 1 minute. Don't burn it!
  4. Once butter and garlic are giving off the beautiful aroma, add in flour and mix it until light golden.
  5. Add half and half, mixed out all of the lumps. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with parsley and add some parmesan cheese if you'd like!
